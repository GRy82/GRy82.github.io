Pool Ball: Billiards Gone Wild

Pool ball is a fast paced game based on the real, live-action "alternative" sport once promulgated in the Western New York region. Before attending devCodeCamp, I took HarvardX's intro to programming course, and made this as my final project. The project was selected to be displayed in the course's project gallery.

It is programmed using the scripting language Lua, and uses the 2d game engine called Love. This was scripted prior to learning many of the good coding habits I now embrace and exhibit. However, I bring attention to this project because it showcases my broad array of skills (all graphics and audio are original content). It's also to showcase my ability to take the vision of a project, and successfully materialize a polished product.

AutonoFit: your time, your resources, your life


Fitness/exercise web application that supplies users with convenient, automatically generated exercise routines. These routines are based on the user's fitness goals, the amount of time they have available, and the equipment they have at their disposal. Generate a one-time workout, or progress through a customized program. This application makes https requests to a REST API that serves as an exercise library. It then uses its categorical criteria to find you the appropriate exercises at appropriately prescribed reps, sets, rest time, and even distance for runners.
